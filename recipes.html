Quick Recipes

Garlic Parmesan Wings

  1. Air-fry wings until crisp.
  2. Toss in melted butter, garlic, and parmesan.
  3. Finish with parsley and cracked pepper.

Smoky Grilled Breast

  1. Marinate with paprika, lemon, and olive oil.
  2. Grill 6–7 min per side.
  3. Rest 5 min; slice over salads or wraps.
